Tomato and Cucumber Salad Recipe

Easy Salad

Delicious Healthy Salad!

Ingredients

  • Cherry tomato
  • Cucumber
  • Dill
  • Purple onion
  • Vinegar
  • Sugar

Steps

  1. Clean the ingredients
  2. Chop chop tomato into half,slice cucumber into smaller pieces, mince dill and onion
  3. Put vinegar into a large bowl, flavor with sugar as preference, and put minced onion in for 5 to 10 minutes
  4. Put tomato, cucumber, and dill into the bowl and keep in the refrigerator for at least 30 minutes
  5. The salad is ready to serve
